Text: Ryan Carlson Date: 18. March 2026 Duration: 3m
Ryan
Carlson
Photographer — Writer — Van Dweller

I live in a van with my partner and our Pomeranian. We left a fixed address in 2024 and haven't had a reason to go back. The Southwest is where we've spent most of our time — Sedona, New Mexico, the Texas hill country — anywhere the light does something interesting and the parking is free.

I shoot on a Fujifilm X-series, almost always in black and white. The camera is how I pay attention. It forces me to slow down and actually look at what's in front of me, which is harder than it sounds when everything is moving.

This journal is a record of that. Not a travel blog. Not an influencer account. Just photographs and the small stories that go with them.
